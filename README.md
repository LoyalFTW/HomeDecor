<h2 style="color: #f90;">üè† What is HomeDecor?</h2>
<p><strong>HomeDecor</strong> is a clean, in-game catalog for WoW Housing that helps you find, track, and plan your builds without digging through websites or spreadsheets. It organizes decor by category, expansion, and zone &mdash; and shows you exactly how to get it.</p>
<hr>
<h2 style="color: #f90;">‚ú® Why You&rsquo;ll Want This</h2>
<p style="font-size: 14px;">Housing collections get big _fast_. HomeDecor is built to make browsing simple: **search** anything, **filter** instantly, and **save** your favorites for later.</p>
<hr>
<h2 style="color: #f90;">üõ†Ô∏è What Does the Addon Do?</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Massive decor catalog</strong> &mdash; browse items with categories, subcategories, and expansions</li>
<li><strong>Smart filters</strong> &mdash; Expansion, Zone, Faction, Category + Subcategory, and more</li>
<li><strong>Search everything</strong> &mdash; titles, sources, vendors, quests, achievements, drops</li>
<li><strong>Collected tracking</strong> &mdash; hide/show collected items (perfect for farming)</li>
<li><strong>Favorites / Saved Items</strong> &mdash; star items and build your own shopping list</li>
<li><strong>Source clarity</strong> &mdash; shows where it comes from (Vendor / Quest / Achievement / Drop / PvP / Profession)</li>
<li><strong>Clickable locations</strong> &mdash; jump to the correct map &amp; coordinates when available</li>
<li><strong>Wowhead links</strong> &mdash; quick copyable links for items, quests, and achievements</li>
<li><strong>Clean UI</strong> &mdash; lightweight, fast scrolling, designed for large datasets</li>
</ul>
